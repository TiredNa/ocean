Clown Fish

If you are looking for a hardy saltwater fish, then clownfish is the fish for you. They are quite small and are not fussy eaters. However, they are territorial.

You might also know them as Nemo, from the popular Disney animated film.

Clownfish are tropical fish found on coral reefs all over around the world. They are orange with white stripes and usually live in anemones.

They love hiding space and will need a few throughout the aquariums, and they prefer a varied diet.

hope that one of these fish will be ideal for your first tank!

There are plenty of clownfish surround the Philippines, in fact the Philippines seems to be a hot zone for a wide variety of clownfish types.

Quick Facts

Other Common Names: Nemo, Anemonefish
Scientific name Amphiprioninae
Family Name: Pomacentridae
Classification Actinopterygii
Distribution Singapore, Malaysia, Sumatra, and southern Thailand
Family Tropical coral reefs
Reproduction Males become female to breed
Size 1.5-4 inches
Diet Omnivore
Color Orange and white, red and white, black and white
Care Level Moderate
Temperament Semi-aggressive
Lifespan 5-6 years
Minimum Tank Size: 20 gallons
Tank Mate Compatibility: Other saltwater reef fish
